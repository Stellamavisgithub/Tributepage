Tribute to Bishop Ezeonyia C.S.Sp

1941-2015

Biography

Vincent Ezeconyia was born on 5th April 1941 at Ukeh, in the diocese of Onitsha, Nigeria. He pronounced his first vows at the Holy Ghost Novitiate Awo-Omamma on 12th February 1962. He was ordained to the priesthood on 3rd August 1968 at Ukeh. He worked as Chaplain of the University of Nigeria Nsukk. He was provincial of Nigeria-East in 1980 when he was elected as Assistant General under the mandate of Fr. Frans Timmermans, CSSp. At the end of his mandate, he went back to Nigeria where he served as Director of the Spiritan Scholasticate, Enugu. In 1990, he was sent to Miami, USA for the service of Haitian refugees. On 2nd April of the same year, he was appointed the bishop of Aba(Nigeria) and ordained on 1st July 1990 at Umuahia Throughout his journey,he accomlished remarkable achievements,from the establishment of vital community programs to the constructions of places of worship that now stands as symbols of hope.
